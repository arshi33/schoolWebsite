ACADEMIC REWARDS


Class I

Following Special Awards will be given twice in the academic year in each section.
  • Perfect Turn Out
  • Maximum Participation In School Activities
  • Most Disciplined Child
  • Knowledge Beyond The text
  • Remarkable Improvement

Following awards will be given annually in each section.
  1. 100% Attendance


    2. Class II to XII
    To Motivate and encourage its students, the school honours them by awarding Academic Prozes, Merit Certificates, Scholar’s Badgees, Scholar’s Gowns and Gold Medals based on the following criteria.

    • PROFICIENCY CERTIFICATE

Classes II & III
  • Minimum A 1 grade in all subjects.
  • Minimum A 2 grade in General Awareness and Value Education.
  • Minimum B grade in Co scholastic Areas.
  • 90% attendance.


  • SCHOLAR’S BADGE

    • Class IV & V
    • Minimum grade point-39.
    • Minimum A 2 grade in all subjects.
    • Minimum A 2 grade in subjects other than the five main subjects.
    • 90% attendance.

    Class VI to VIII
    1. Minimum grade point-46.
    2. Minimum A 2 grade in all subjects.
    3. Minimum A 2 grade in subjects other than the six main subjects.
    4. 90% attendance.

      5. Class IX to XII
      • Minimum grade point-38.
      • Minimum A 2 grade in all subjects.
      • Minimum A 2 grade in subjects other than the five main subjects.
      • 90% attendance.

    5. SCHOLAR'S GOWN / BLAZER

      6. Scholar's Gown shall be awarded to a student who earns Scholar's Badge for three consecutive years.
      (Class VI and above)

    6. GOLD MEDAL Gold Medal is awarded to students who have been scholar for six years in succession
      (class IX onwards) The gold medallist will also be awarded a Blue Blazer.

    7. CLASS TOPPERS & SECTION TOPPERS
      8. Classes II to XII These awards are based on aggregate marks obtained in a year.
    8. SUBJECT TOPPER'S CERTIFICATE
      9. Classes IV to XII
      Will be awarded to the student who has scored highest in individual examinable subjects. The minimum percentage required is 85%.

    9. MR M. M. SINGH MEMORIAL AWARD
      10. For outstanding performance in Class XI-Science Stream.
    10. PROF. SADA SHIV BHAGAT 'PRIDE OF THE SCHOOL GOLD MEDAL
      11. This award is given to one boy and one girl from among the first three position holders in each section of Classes VI to XI by judging them on their academic, co-curricular and sports achievements.
    11. ANAND GUPTA MEMORIAL AWARD
      12. This award of Rs. 10,000 is given to a student who scores highest marks in Physics in class XI.
    12. MERIT CERTIFICATE
      13. Merit certificate is awarded to the students who:
      • Attain 100 % attendance.
      • Excel in Games and Sports.
      • Excel in Co-curricular / Cultural Activities.


    NOTE
    1. For the award of Scholar's Badge, the child must get minimum B grade in Health and Physical Education, Art & Work Experience, Music and Dance etc.
    2. Children using Unfair Means in examination will not be eligible for any academic award.
    3. For the consideration of any academic award a student must appear in all examinations.
