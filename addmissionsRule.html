ADMISSION RULES

Registration


Registration form is made available on the school's website. The form has to be filled online and then the print out of the form is to be deposited in the school's Students' Section. Processing fee shall be charged which shall be paid at the State Bank of India, Free will School RK Puram Branch. The admission shall depend upon the availability of seats and according to the norms of CBSE and DPSS New Delhi.
The schedule for registration is as follows:

Class Prep. Jr.
First week of December onwards every year.
Class Prep Sr. to Class IX
As and when notification is issued (Normally first week of April every year).
Class XI
After the declaration of Class X (AISSE) result.
Classes X and XII
No admission as per CBSE directives (clause 7.3 of CBSE Examination Bye Laws).
Eligibility
Minimum age limit for Class Prep Jr. is 3 years as on April 1 of the academic year in which the admission is being sought.
For subsequent classes the eligible age will be reckoned again with reference to April 1, with proportionate increase over 3 years.


Admission Procedure

Class Prep. Jr.
Criteria for Admission to Class Prep Junior will be followed as per the directives issued by the Ministry of Human Resources Development. Class Prep. Sr.
Observation - Cum - Interaction
Admission will be granted based on observation-cum-interaction session. Presence of both the parents during the session is mandatory. Parents will have to bring the Admit and Identity cards, on the date and time mentioned on the cards, when they come for the observation-cum-interaction.
List of successful candidates will be put up on the School's Notice Board and also posted on the School's Website. The parents of these candidates will be required to contact the school office for the completion of admission formalities.
Classes I to IX
Written Test and Interaction or as decided by school administration/ Free Will School RK Puram Noida/ CBSE Delhi.

Normal Procedure

From Class I onwards, a formal written test is conducted in English Language (to check the Linguistic Proficiency), Mathematical Ability, Science/ Environmental Studies and General Awareness for assessing the suitability for admission to the next class.
Candidates have to bring the Admit and Identity cards, on the date and time mentioned on the cards, when they come for the written test.
List of successful candidates is posted on the School's Notice Board / School Website.
The candidates who clear the written test have to come to the Administrative Office of the School for an interaction session along with the parents.

NOTE: No candidate is admitted to the school without clearing the observation-cum-interaction / written test and interaction. The parents of candidates who are selected for admission are required to contact the School Administrative Office for completion of the admission formalities (if notification given on website).

Rules concerning Withdrawal

  1. A clear calendar months' notice must be given in writing or a month's fees In lieu of such notice must be given before a pupil is withdrawn from the school.
  2. Those who leave the school in the month of May must in all cases pay the fees for the month of June.
  3. Transfer or withdrawal certificate will not be issued until all dues of the school are settled.
  4. Students can be asked to leave the school on the following grounds.
  5. Disciplinary
  6. Unsatisfactory progress in scholastic and co-scholastic areas.
  7. A child failing twice in the same class will not be permitted to continue his/her studies in the school.
