SCHOOL UNIFORM

SUMMER




Classes Prep Jr. and Prep Sr.

BOYS

White half sleeved shirt (with proper collar) and white shorts (without zip). White vest should always be worn under the shirt. Bottle green school tie, bottle green belt with school emblem, white socks (long) with two green stripes, black velcro shoes and bottle green putka for Sikh boys.

GIRLS

White half sleeved shirt (with proper collar) and white single pleated knee-length skirt. Bottle green school tie, bottle green belt with school emblem, white socks (long) with two green stripes, black velcro shoes and bottle green ribbon/band.

There is no badge for classes Prep Jr. and Prep Sr.

CLASSES I to XII

BOYS

White half-sleeved shirt (with proper collar), white shorts (not more than 2" above the knees) for classes I to VIII, white straight trousers with two plates for classes IX to XII, bottle green school tie and house badge, bottle green belt with school emblem, white socks (long) with two green stripes, plain black shoes with laces (non-leather medium toe) and bottle green putka or turban for Sikh boys. White vest should always be worn under the shirt.

GIRLS

White half-sleeved shirt (with proper collar), white single pleated skirt (till the knees), bottle green school tie and house badge, bottle green belt with school emblem, white socks (long) with two green stripes, plain black shoes with strap and buckle (non-leather medium toe) and bottle green ribbon/band.

WINTER

Classes Prep Jr. and Prep Sr.

BOYS

White full sleeved shirt (with proper collar), grey worsted trousers with elastic waist-band (without zip and buttons), plain bottle green V-neck pullover, bottle green blazer (single breast) with school monogram, bottle green school tie, bottle green belt with school emblem, grey woollen socks (long) with two green stripes, bottle green woollen cap, black velcro shoes and bottle green putka for Sikh boys.

GIRLS

White full sleeved shirt (with proper collar), grey worsted trousers with elastic waist-band (without zip and buttons), plain bottle green V-neck pullover, bottle green blazer (single breast) with school monogram, bottle green school tie, bottle green belt with school emblem, grey woollen socks (long) with two green stripes, bottle green woollen cap, black velcro shoes and bottle green ribbon/hair band.

There is no badge for classes Prep Jr. and Prep Sr.

CLASSES I to XII

BOYS

White full sleeved shirt with proper collar, bottle-green tie and house badge, bottle-green belt with school emblem, grey worsted straight trousers, plain bottle green V-neck pullover (sleeves optional), bottle green blazer (single breast) with school monogram, grey woollen socks (long) with two green stripes, plain black shoes with laces (non-leather medium toe) and bottle green putka/turban for Sikh boys.

GIRLS

White full sleeved shirt with proper collar, bottle-green tie and house badge, bottle-green belt with school emblem, grey worsted straight trousers for girls of Classes I to III, grey worsted single pleated skirt/straight trousers for girls of Classes IV to XII, plain bottle green V-neck pullover (sleeves optional), bottle green blazer (single breast) with school monogram, grey woollen socks (long) with two green stripes and plain black shoes with strap and buckle (non-leather medium toe).

SPORTS UNIFORM

House T-shirt and black shorts (as per approved pattern), white socks with two green stripes and white tennis shoes are to be worn for matches/tournaments.

KINDLY NOTE:

The school has no authorized tailor shop. The parents are free to buy the uniform from any shop. However the exact uniform specifications as given above are to be followed before purchasing the uniform, the school has fixed the following fabrics.

1. “White Rose” brand of Mafatlal for shirts

2. “Officer's Pride brand of S. Kumar for shorts and trousers

3. Graviera Mills grey worsted for trousers and skirts (Shade No. 2)

4. Diamond mills (quality No 5151) for blazer



Rules for Uniform

1. The students should take pride in wearing the school uniform.

2. Students are expected to attend school in neat and well ironed, complete uniform and boys with short and decent haircut. In case of non-compliance, they will not be allowed to attend classes and parents shall be called to take them home. Kindly ensure that the instructions are followed in all seriousness to avoid any inconvenience to your good self later.

3. STUDENTS ARE EXPECTED TO ENTER SCHOOL CAMPUS ONLY IN SCHOOL UNIFORM AND NOT IN CIVIL OUTFIT EVEN FOR PURPOSE OTHER THAN ACADEMIC.

4. They must also be in uniform and when representing the school in any Inter school events or programs.

5. Girls must wear knee-length skirt.

6. The trousers should be straight having two pleats. Fancy trousers are not permitted.

7. Tennis shoes must be worn only for games or on PT day. However, on all formal occasions, students are expected to wear black shoes.

8. Parents must ensure that their wards wear only white under clothes (white slips for girls and white vests for boys). It is mandatory for girls to wear white bloomers under their skirts/tunics.

9. Girls must tie their hair neatly in two plaits with bottle green hair band/ribbon (No other colour is allowed)



10. No fancy hair clips or jewellery is allowed.
